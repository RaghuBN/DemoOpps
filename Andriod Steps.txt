    1.Java Setups
    2.Android Studio downloads
    	https://developer.android.com/studio?

    3.Connect mobie through Vyser(Vyser Download and install)
    4.https://developer.android.com/studio/command-line/sdkmanager

      	C:\AndriodSDK\tools\bin>sdkmanager "platform-tools" "platforms;android-28"
    5.Set Environment variable path for Andriod SDK
       C:\AndriodSDK\platform-tools
    6.C:\Users\bnuft>adb devices
    7.Make Device Ready
        Enable Develope mode
        Make USB Debugging ON
